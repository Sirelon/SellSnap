SellSnap

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SellSnap handles information when you use the SellSnap mobile, desktop, or web app. SellSnap helps sellers create marketplace listings from photos, notes, and optional account data.

SellSnap is not OLX. OLX account sign-in, account data, and listing publication are provided by OLX and remain subject to OLX's own terms and privacy practices.

Who We Are

SellSnap is provided by the SellSnap developer. For privacy questions, data requests, or deletion requests, contact sasha.sirelon@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, SellSnap may process the following information:

  • Photos you select or capture for a listing, including converted image files generated by the app.
  • Seller notes, item descriptions, generated titles, generated descriptions, suggested prices, categories, attributes, and listing metadata.
  • OLX authorization data, including OAuth callback state and access or refresh tokens needed to connect your OLX account.
  • OLX profile information returned by OLX, such as your seller name and email address.
  • Location coordinates if you choose to use device location so SellSnap can suggest a nearby OLX listing city or district.
  • Audio recordings or live microphone audio if you use the speech-to-text proof-of-concept feature.
  • Basic technical data generated by app requests, such as request status, errors, and service responses.

How We Use Information

SellSnap uses information to:

  • Generate listing titles, descriptions, prices, categories, and attributes.
  • Upload listing photos and obtain public image URLs needed for listing generation and publication.
  • Connect to your OLX account, read your OLX seller profile, and publish listings when you request it.
  • Suggest listing location values based on your device location when you request location assistance.
  • Transcribe audio when you use the transcription feature.
  • Maintain app session state, troubleshoot errors, and protect the app from misuse.

Third-Party Services

SellSnap shares information with third-party services only as needed to provide the app's features:

  • OpenAI may receive listing photos, seller notes, generated listing context, and audio submitted for transcription or AI-assisted listing generation.
  • Supabase may store uploaded listing photos and authentication data used by SellSnap's storage integration.
  • OLX may receive your authorization request, profile requests, listing content, image URLs, location data, category and attribute data, and publish requests.

Permissions

SellSnap asks for device permissions only when needed for a feature:

  • Camera access is used to capture listing photos.
  • Photo or file access is used to select listing photos.
  • Location access is used to suggest a nearby OLX listing location.
  • Microphone access is used for recording and transcription features.

Storage, Retention, and Deletion

OLX tokens and session data are stored locally by the app so you can stay connected. You can disconnect from OLX in the app where that option is available, and token data may also be cleared when authorization fails or expires.

Uploaded listing photos may remain in SellSnap's storage provider until they are no longer needed for app functionality, operational maintenance, or legal reasons. To request deletion of data associated with your use of SellSnap, contact sasha.sirelon@gmail.com.

Security

SellSnap uses service APIs and local app storage to process information needed for listing creation and publication. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, so SellSnap cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

SellSnap is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through SellSnap, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Your Choices

  • You can use guest mode to create listing text without connecting an OLX account.
  • You can decline optional permissions, though related features may not work.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting sasha.sirelon@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when SellSnap changes or when legal, operational, or platform requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page shows when this version became effective.
